Roadmap at a glance
  • Quick Response AI for email and chat with draft-only, approval-required, and safe auto-send modes.
  • Unified action register that pulls tasks out of email, Slack, Teams, PDFs, and meetings.
  • Follow-up autopilot, VIP routing, commitment tracking, and escalation rules for daily office execution.
  • Meeting-to-execution pipeline with owners, due dates, reminders, and role-based priorities.
  • Revenue and risk cockpit with CRM next-best-action, pipeline risk, customer health, and inventory alerts.
  • Enterprise hardening with audit trail, explainability, role permissions, and safe automation controls.
  • Launch tooling with onboarding analytics, pricing tiers, and trial-to-paid conversion, while keeping free-tier access.
